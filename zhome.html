WELCOME TO ZCOINs ECOSYSTEM

ZCOINs Token (ZCO) is an ERC-20 token designed for the chess community and other gaming platforms. ZCOINs Token (ZCO) combines frictionless yield and deflationary mechanisms to reward holders. Hold and Earn.

ZCO works by applying 2% transaction fee on sells and buys respectively, where 1% on every transaction is burnt forever and the remaining 1% is distributed among holders. This means that over time, ZCO holders will gain a larger percentage of the total market cap. The initial total supply of ZCO was 1,000,000,000 and is decreasing daily due to burns.

Trade Enhancement

ZCOINs encourages buys and sells through enhanced innovations in the smart contract that allows certain addresses, like the Uniswap pool or exchange wallets, to be blocked from earning fess. This implementation allows 100% of the fees generated go to holders of the token. The percentage of fees earned is based on the percentage of individual holdings. This encourages buys and sells and also eradicate impatial buy high.

Partnership and Chess Event organising

ZCOINs tokens is developed to serve as a decentralized payment token among the chess community and other useful platforms. Chess events both online and physical will be improved by Partnering with event organisers in order to improve the payment of players, discount allowance among event organisers and also for partcipation benefit of ZCO holders all around the globe.

Fast and Reliable Payment

Unlike the centralized finance where longer days payment (est. 7 to 24 working days ) process and high charge rate cost, ZCO proof to be upgraded by using decentralized finance to make prize payment to event winners within 12 to 15 blocks confirmation (est. 1 to 24 minutes ) only at the expense of gas fee. This will make it easier to register for online tournament around the globe because of ZCOINs currency pair.
